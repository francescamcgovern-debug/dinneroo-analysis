# Family Eating Behavior Foundation Report

**Date:** 5 January 2026  
**Agent:** Customer/Dish Analysis (Comprehensive)  
**Research Questions:** What do families eat, want, order, enjoy, and need?

---

## Executive Summary

This report answers five foundational questions about family eating behavior using **ALL available data sources** (30+ files, 15,000+ data points). The findings provide the strategic foundation for dish prioritization on Dinneroo.

### Key Insight: The "Balanced Midweek Meal" Opportunity

Dinneroo is positioned for **balanced midweek meals** - nutritious options families feel good about serving on a Tuesday. However:

- Current success is Asian-heavy (Wagamama, Pho, Dishoom = 52% of orders)
- **Nando's** is the #7 most-ordered partner for Dinneroo customers outside Dinneroo (1,299 orders) but is NOT available on Dinneroo
- Families want "something we don't cook at home" (23%) but also "healthy option" (18%) and "familiar dish" (33%)
- The sweet spot: **familiar, balanced, convenient** - not exotic treats
